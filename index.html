Understanding Cannabis and Its Components

Cannabis contains over 100 active compounds, known as cannabinoids, with THC (tetrahydrocannabinol) and CBD (cannabidiol) being the most well-known. THC is the compound responsible for the psychoactive effects, while CBD is non-psychoactive and often associated with therapeutic benefits. Visiting a reputable herb shop or herbal medicine store allows you to find products tailored to your needs, whether for relaxation, pain management, or general wellness.

Risks Associated With Cannabis Use

While cannabis has many potential benefits, there are risks that should not be overlooked. THC can cause impaired memory, coordination issues, and altered judgment, which may increase the risk of accidents. Long-term heavy use has been linked to potential mental health concerns, including anxiety or exacerbation of pre-existing conditions.
